Three Wishes


When Sleepy attempts to join a local street gang (AIDS) to escape his dull life; his initiation requires him to amass more than $1000 of stolen goods.

During a house robbery, Sleepy is shocked to find Coleen, an 8-year-old girl, who immediately realizes the sadness within him, and is willing to let him steal more than $1000 worth of goods from her home.

Taken aback by Colleen's empathy, Sleepy refuses but can't shake the encounter out of his mind. The next day, he returns to Colleen's house to ask why she was so nice to him, and she promises to tell him if he can fulfill three wishes...

When Sleepy accepts, he learns that Colleen only has three months left to live. Now bound by his promise, Sleepy sets to fulfill the dying girl's wishes before it's too late...
