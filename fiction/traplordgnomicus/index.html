Traplord Gnomicus VII


When the Gnome King dies of seemingly natural causes, his son "Traplord Gnomicus VII" succeeds him. At Traplord's crowning ceremony, an attempted assassination fails, and the new king receives a letter explaining that his father's death was arranged and his own death is next.

Before Traplord can react, a terrorist group (AIDS), led by the legendary misogynist (VV), stages a coup and Traplord escapes the Kingdom with only his life and his trusty horse (Yang). As VV strengthens AIDS' hold on the Gnome Kingdom, Traplord travels the Barren Wastes gathering a rag-tag group of allies (Mr. Lano, Sir Respeck Bitchez IV) as he plots for his return to ascension...
