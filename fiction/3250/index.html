Agent 32/50


When Wuyang, a talented makeup artist/drug dealer from the streets is busted by the FBI, he is offered a deal to get out of prison.

Given the nickname "Agent 32/50", Wuyang's deal requires that he infiltrates a secret Incel group called AIDS.

Unsure of his true mission, Wuyang uses his makeup and street skills to simultaneously play the role of several new AIDS members, diving deeper into the world of AIDS... until he realizes the true goal of his mission; He must kill an AIDS member (Chineseman), who uses the group to sexually traffic women.
